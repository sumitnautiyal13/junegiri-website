How we handle your data, simply put.

Last updated: 1 May 2026

JuneGiri Farms Private Limited ("we", "us", "JuneGiri") operates junegiriyatra.com and junegirifarms.com. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What we collect

When you book, message, or subscribe with us, we collect:

  • Contact details: name, phone, email, WhatsApp number
  • Booking details: dates, room preference, guest count, dietary preferences
  • Payment info: handled by Razorpay — we don't store card details
  • ID for check-in: Aadhaar / passport (mandatory under Indian law for hospitality)
  • Website analytics: anonymous pageview data via Google Analytics

2. How we use it

  • To confirm and manage your booking
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, and post-stay follow-ups
  • To meet legal/tax compliance (GST, hospitality records)
  • To send our newsletter (only if you opted in — unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve the website and services

3. Who we share with

We do not sell your data. We share only with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing
  • Google Workspace — for email storage
  • WhatsApp Business — for messaging
  • Government authorities — only when legally required (e.g., hotel ID submission to local police)

4. How long we keep it

Booking records: 7 years (legal/tax requirement). Newsletter list: until you unsubscribe. Website analytics: 26 months (Google default).

5. Your rights

You can ask us, at any time, to:

  • See what data we hold about you
  • Correct anything that's wrong
  • Delete your data (subject to legal retention)
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter

Email junegirifarms@gmail.com and we'll respond within 7 working days.

6. Cookies

We use minimal first-party cookies for site functionality and Google Analytics for anonymous traffic data. You can disable cookies in your browser without losing site access.

7. Changes to this policy

We update this policy when our practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version.
