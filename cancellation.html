Cancellation & Refund Policy

Clear cancellation timelines for stays, retreats, and yatras.

Home / Cancellation
Last updated: 1 May 2026

We keep cancellation simple and fair. Refunds are processed within 7 working days to the original payment method.

1. Homestay & cottage stays

For Jungle View Room, River Cottage, and Farm Stay Suite bookings:

Cancellation windowRefund
15+ days before check-in100% refund
14 – 8 days before check-in75% refund
7 – 3 days before check-in50% refund
Less than 3 days / no-showNo refund

45+ days before departure100% refund (less ₹2,500 admin)

4. Corporate retreats

Custom-quoted corporate offsites have a separate cancellation schedule outlined in the proposal. Generally: 50% non-refundable on confirmation, balance refundable up to 21 days before start date.

5. Force majeure

For cancellations caused by events beyond our control — natural disasters, pandemics, government-mandated road closures, political unrest — we will:

  • Offer to reschedule at no extra cost (subject to availability), OR
  • Issue a travel credit valid for 12 months, OR
  • Refund minus actual non-recoverable costs paid to third parties

6. How to cancel

Send a cancellation request via WhatsApp at +91 98738 97652 or email junegirifarms@gmail.com. Include your booking reference. Cancellation is effective from the time we receive your message.

7. Refund timeline

  • UPI / bank transfer: 3–5 working days
  • Credit / debit card (Razorpay): 7–10 working days
  • Refunds always go to the original payment method
