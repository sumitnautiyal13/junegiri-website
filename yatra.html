11-day Char Dham sample itinerary

Day by day, how the yatra unfolds.

01Arrival

Haridwar / Dehradun → JuneGiri Farms

Airport pickup. 50 km drive to JuneGiri (Rishikesh). Rest, dinner, briefing on yatra logistics.

02Day 1

JuneGiri → Barkot (200 km)

Drive to Barkot via Mussoorie. Halt at Kempty Falls. Overnight at hotel in Barkot.

03Day 2

Yamunotri Darshan → Barkot

Drive to Janki Chatti (45 km). 6 km trek to Yamunotri. Darshan, aarti. Return overnight Barkot.

04Day 3

Barkot → Uttarkashi (100 km)

Drive to Uttarkashi via Tehri. Visit Kashi Vishwanath temple. Overnight hotel.

05Day 4

Gangotri Darshan → Uttarkashi

Drive to Gangotri (100 km). Darshan at source of Ganga. Return overnight Uttarkashi.

06Day 5

Uttarkashi → Guptkashi (220 km)

Long drive day. Halt at Tehri Lake viewpoint. Overnight Guptkashi.

07Day 6

Kedarnath Darshan

Helicopter from Phata (5 min) OR 16 km trek from Gaurikund. Darshan, return Guptkashi by evening.

08Day 7

Guptkashi → Badrinath (200 km)

Drive through Joshimath. Overnight at Badrinath.

09Day 8

Badrinath Darshan + Mana Village

Morning darshan. Visit Mana (last village before Tibet border). Return overnight Badrinath.

10Day 9

Badrinath → JuneGiri Farms (280 km)

Drive back to JuneGiri. Rest, hot meal, Pahadi dinner.

11Departure

JuneGiri → Haridwar / Dehradun

Late breakfast, drop to airport / station. Yatra complete.
