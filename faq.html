Location & Getting Here

Where exactly is JuneGiri Farms?

House No-85, Post-Maral, Yamkeshwar block, Pauri Garhwal, Uttarakhand 249304. We're at the edge of Rajaji National Park, about 45 km from Haridwar railway station and 7 km from the Neelkanth Mahadev Temple.

What's the nearest airport / railway?

Nearest airport: Jolly Grant, Dehradun (75 km, 2.5 hr drive). Nearest railway: Haridwar Junction (45 km, 1.5 hr drive). Rishikesh railway station is also 35 km away.

Do you offer airport / railway pickup?

Yes. Haridwar railway pickup is free for all guests. Dehradun airport pickup is ₹1,500 extra, one way, for up to 4 passengers.

Can we self-drive?

Yes, but the last 4 km is hilly with narrow turns. Sedan-class cars and SUVs are fine. We have free parking for up to 8 cars.

Rooms & Stay

What's the cheapest room?

The Jungle View Room at ₹2,500/night (twin sharing, includes breakfast + dinner + morning yoga).

How many rooms do you have?

3 room types, 12 keys total. Jungle View (8 rooms), River Cottage (2), Farm Stay Suite (2).

Is hot water available 24×7?

Yes. All rooms have solar-heated water with electric backup. Available 24×7.

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Yes — 50 Mbps fibre across the property. Speeds drop in heavy monsoon but it generally works fine for video calls and streaming.

Is it heated in winter?

Rooms have electric heaters available December–February. We also provide hot water bags and extra blankets.

What are check-in / check-out times?

Check-in 12:00 PM. Check-out 11:00 AM. Early check-in / late check-out is usually possible if the next guest hasn't booked — just ask.

Food & Dietary

What's the food like?

Pahadi vegetarian, farm-to-table. Most ingredients are grown on our 0.4-acre organic plot or sourced from named farmers within Yamkeshwar block. Three meals a day — breakfast, lunch, dinner. Specific dishes vary by season.

Is the kitchen vegetarian?

Yes, strictly vegetarian. We don't serve meat, fish, or eggs in the kitchen. Eggs are available at breakfast on request (cooked separately).

What about Jain / vegan / gluten-free?

All three are easily accommodated — just tell us at booking. Jain food (no onion/garlic/root veg), vegan options (almond milk, coconut yoghurt), and gluten-free meals are all standard.

Can kids get plain dal-rice / dosa?

Always. We keep idli, dosa, dal-rice, and curd-rice on rotation specifically for kids and elders.

Family, Kids & Pets

Is JuneGiri kid-friendly?

Very. Children under 5 stay free (no extra bed). We have cribs, high-chairs, kid-safe trails, and a small play area. The Farm Stay Suite sleeps 4 and is built for families with kids.

Is it elderly-friendly?

Main rooms and dining are step-free. We don't have lifts, but no room requires climbing more than one flight. Doctor on call within 15 minutes, pharmacy 6 km away.

Can I bring my dog?

Yes, one medium-sized dog per booking on prior intimation. Fenced outdoor area, dog-friendly dining, and dog beds available. ₹500/night cleaning fee.

Booking, Payment & Cancellation

How do I book?

Easiest is WhatsApp at +91 98738 97652. Tell us your dates, room preference, and number of guests. We confirm availability within an hour, then a 30% deposit holds your booking.

What's the cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 14 days before check-in. 50% refund 14–7 days before. No refund within 7 days. Char Dham yatra has a separate policy — full refund up to 30 days before departure.

How do I pay?

UPI, bank transfer, or credit/debit card via Razorpay invoice. We send the payment link via WhatsApp.

Do you provide a GST invoice?

Yes. All invoices include our GSTIN. We can split for corporate billing on request.

Yoga, Retreats & Yatra

Is yoga included with my stay?

Yes. Morning Hatha at 6:30 AM and evening Yin at 5:30 PM are free for all guests. Private sessions are ₹500/person/session.

How do retreats differ from a regular stay?

Retreats are 5 or 7-day fixed-schedule programmes with twice-daily yoga, meditation, satsang, optional silence, and curated meals. Regular stays are flexible — you can do as much or as little yoga as you want.

Is the Char Dham yatra all-inclusive?

Yes. Transport, all stays, all meals, all permits, darshan assistance, doctor on call — everything included in the package price. Only personal expenses (puja, additional helicopters, tips) are extra.

Weather, Wildlife & Safety

What's the best time to visit?

Honestly — anytime. Mar–May: warm, perfect for forest walks. Jun–Aug: monsoon, lush and dramatic. Sep–Nov: clear skies, best for yatra. Dec–Feb: cold and quiet, fireplace season.

Are there wild animals around?

Yes. We're at the edge of Rajaji National Park — elephants, leopards, sambar, monkeys, and 300+ bird species. We have safe walking trails and brief all guests on basics. We've had zero incidents in 4 years.

Is it safe for solo female travellers?

Yes, very. We get 30–40% solo female travellers every season. All-female staff in housekeeping, well-lit common areas, and a small property where you're never far from help.
