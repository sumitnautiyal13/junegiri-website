Good questions

Before you join.

Is this a timeshare?

No. There's no shared ownership, no fixed week, and no annual maintenance fee. It's simply a prepaid pass: pay once, enjoy free stays and member privileges over your chosen term.

Are there really no annual fees?

Really. You pay once. Unlike most holiday clubs, we don't bill a yearly subscription or maintenance fee, that's the whole point.

When can I use my free stays?

On Sunday-Thursday nights in regular season, subject to availability and the blackout dates above. Weekend and peak dates are always available at your member rate.

What if I can't use a stay?

Gift it. Unused stays are transferable to friends and family, and every pass includes one free expiry extension if life gets in the way.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, a full refund within the first 10 days, no questions asked. After that, a fair pro-rated cancellation applies and your unused stays remain transferable.

How do I book once I'm a member?

Just WhatsApp us your dates. We hold your room and confirm, no portals, no booking fees, no chasing "availability."
